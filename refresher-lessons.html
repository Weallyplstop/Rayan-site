Refresher lessons that rebuild confidence

Perfect if you’ve taken a driving break, switched countries, or simply want a safety tune-up in automatic or manual cars.

Choose the refresher you need

We customise each block to your driving history and nerves.

Confidence builder

1–2 sessions covering cockpit drill, urban roads, and hazard perception—ideal after years away from the wheel.

UK conversion

For international drivers: left-hand lane discipline, roundabouts, and UK-specific signage/passing laws.

Licence tune-up

Mock-test style drives with in-depth debriefs plus focus on manoeuvres, eco-driving, and DVSA safety questions.

What we’ll cover

Pick any mix—we’ll log your goals in our lesson tracker.

Modern car tech
Parking sensors, adaptive cruise, EV regen modes, and infotainment safety.
Town & city driving
Rush-hour spacing, cyclists, bus lanes, and lane discipline.
Night & weather
Headlight etiquette, demisting, and planning for rain or fog.
Parking & manoeuvres
Parallel, bay, and forward bay parking plus slow-speed control.
Motorway/dual carriageway
Overtaking, smart-motorway signage, and breakdown drills.
Confidence coaching
Breathing techniques, commentary driving, and debrief notes you can keep.
