New driver pathway

From first ignition to DVSA pass, we guide beginners with clear milestones, practice plans, and supportive feedback.

Three-stage framework

Structured progress keeps nerves low and motivation high.

Stage 1 · Foundations

Vehicle controls, moving off, stopping smoothly, early junctions, and mirror discipline on quiet estates.

Stage 2 · Traffic ready

Meeting traffic, roundabouts, lane selection, hazard perception drills, and eco-driving habits.

Stage 3 · Test polish

Mock tests, manoeuvres, motorway taster, show-me/tell-me questions, and nerves coaching.

Focus areas

Auto and manual learners both complete these modules.

Lesson journals
