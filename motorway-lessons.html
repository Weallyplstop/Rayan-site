Session flow

We keep tuition predictable so you always know the aim.

1. Brief & route plan
Review motorway diagrams, weather, and plan rest points.
2. Instructor-led demo
Watch how we merge, maintain 2-second rule, and set cruise control.
3. Coached drive
You take the wheel with guided prompts for anticipation and speed management.
4. Debrief & homework
Receive a WhatsApp summary with key wins plus focus areas for regular driving.
