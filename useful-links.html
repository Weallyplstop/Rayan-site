How we use these resources in lessons

  • Every intensive plan includes theory mock tracking—send us your multiple-choice scores so we can tackle weak topics in-car.
  • We’ll book motorway or night lessons around your practical date to mirror “show me / tell me” prompts.
  • Manual learners get extra clutch-control drills ahead of practical day, while automatic learners prep eco-driving and awareness questions.
