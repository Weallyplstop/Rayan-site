Rayan’s story

From nervous learner to specialist instructor.

Rayan trained on the same local Coventry roads before qualifying as an ADI. After supporting friends through manual retests, he launched RDS to provide transparent pricing, flexible scheduling, and accessible automatic options. Today, RDS delivers 1:1 lessons, mock tests, and motorway refreshers tailored to each learner’s timeline.

  • 2018
    Qualified as DVSA Approved Driving Instructor and launched RDS.
  • 2020
    Added automatic vehicles and WhatsApp booking for faster communication.
  • 2022
    Expanded to Bedworth, Nuneaton, Warwick, and Leamington with flexible pick-up points.
  • 2024
    Introduced dual-transmission intensive plans and structured mock-test reports.
