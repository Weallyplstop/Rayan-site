Automatic lessons that fast-track calm driving

Ideal if you want clutch-free journeys, have mobility considerations, or simply prefer focusing on road craft instead of gear changes.

How our automatic lessons run

Structured, feedback-rich sessions tailored to your diary.

Calm onboarding

We begin with cockpit drill, reference points, and smooth acceleration/braking so you’re confident within the first lesson.

Route layering

Progress from quiet estates to Coventry ring-road multi-lane systems, practising eco-driving, planning and positioning.

Mock-test debriefs

Full DVSA-style assessments with annotated score sheets and WhatsApp summaries for home practice.

What you’ll master

Automatic doesn’t mean “basic”—we refine every advanced skill the test expects.

Anticipation & planning
Mirror/Signal/Manoeuvre routines with commentary driving to predict hazards.
Complex junctions
Stoke Aldermoor roundabouts, Tollbar End upgrades, and city-centre one-way grids.
Eco-driving techniques
Feathered throttle, regenerative braking (EV), and speed-limit compliance for smooth rides.
Reverse manoeuvres
Bay parking, parallel parking, and forward bay exits using easy-to-remember reference points.
Night & wet-weather driving
High-beam etiquette, stopping distances, and using wiper/defog systems confidently.
